AUGUST-SEPTEMBER:

Shelter-In-Place orders lifted, businesses reopening.

The government has made the decision to life the sheltering orders and reopen essential businesses. People are encouraged to continue to take preventative measures by distancing socially, wearing masks and avoiding unnecessary exposure.

People emerge from their homes, begin returning to work and their usual daily lives.

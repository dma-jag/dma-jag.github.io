GLITCH IMAGES

The purpose of this project was to take already existing images or photographs and'mosh' or distort their raw information using programs not intented for editing photographs such as Audacity and Textedit.

This series of photos are from one of my favorite cinematic worlds created by Ridley Scott, Alien. The first two images were created using audacity, the third with TextEdit, each staying true to the suspenseful nature of the Alien series by furthering the unknown with distortion and repitition effects.

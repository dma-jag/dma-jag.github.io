JUNE-JULY:

Shelter-In-Place orders lifted, businesses reopening.

Everyones efforts to try and mitigate the spread of the virus seem to be working slowly but surely. Cases are no longer rising as much, and cases are beginning to level off.

Local officials made the decision to extend the Shelter-In-Place order for the first few weeks of April. A decision has also been made to close down schools and non-essential institutions briefly, in order to prevent any unnecessary contact between individuals.

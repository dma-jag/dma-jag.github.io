The populations are growing worried as their loved ones are dying. They attempt to begin taking the recommended measures of social distancing, sheltering in place, and wearing protective masks but hospitals are over capacity and people are unable to be treated.

The number of cases are no longer being tracked. Death toll reaches unimaginable numbers.

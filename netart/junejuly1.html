JUNE-JULY:

Shelter-In-Place orders lifted, businesses reopening.

Cases are dropping fast, not reaching previous daily records. More and more people are able to be treated as hospitals are no longer over crowded. The population seems to understand what is going on, and understnds what part they need to play in order for this virus to pass.

Government officials have made the decision to reopen schools and businesses, but encourage people to stay vigilant and wear protective masks.

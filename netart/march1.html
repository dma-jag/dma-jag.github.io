Local officials order residents to Shelter-In-Place

The population needs time to adjust to these sudden changes to their lives. Cases slowly rise at a less than alarming rate.

Local officials encourage the population to Shelter-In-Place in order to prevent the rapid spread of the virus and help flatten out the growing curve.
